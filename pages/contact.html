Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental United States. Express shipping options are available at checkout for 1-2 business day delivery. International shipping times vary by location but generally take 7-14 business days.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items in original condition with tags attached. Returns are free for customers in the United States. To initiate a return, please visit our Returns page or contact our customer service team.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also log into your account on our website to view your order status and tracking details at any time.

Do you offer size exchanges?

Yes, we offer free size exchanges for all domestic orders. Simply initiate a return and select "exchange" as your reason. You can choose your new size, and we'll ship it to you as soon as we receive your original item.

How do I care for my t-shirts?

For best results, machine wash cold with like colors, use mild detergent, and tumble dry low. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners as they can break down the fabric over time. For graphic tees, turn inside out before washing to preserve the design.
